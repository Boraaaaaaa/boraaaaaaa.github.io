Minecraft UUID
Why is this needed?

To prevent piracy, the mod requires a UUID to be entered (every Minecraft account has a UUID, i.e. a unique id). This is to ensure that only the person who purchased the mod can use it.

If you would like to use the mod on more than 1 account, or want to switch your UUID later, then don't worry, you can do that after signing up.

Your UUID will never be known to anyone other than you, or us (the developers). For more information, please see our privacy policy and terms of service.
